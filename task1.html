Task 1 – Variables & Operators

This task introduces variables, data types, and operators in JavaScript.
You’ll perform calculations and string concatenation, then print results using console.log().

Instructions

  • Declare three variables of different types: string, number, and boolean.
  • Perform arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Concatenate two strings and print them in the console.
  • Use console.log() to display all results.

Open your browser’s console (F12 → Console tab) to see the output.
